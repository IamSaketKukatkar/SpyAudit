Frequently Asked Questions

How does SpyAudit detect threats?

SpyAudit uses advanced threat intelligence from VirusTotal to analyze websites in real-time. We scan for known malware, phishing attempts, and other security threats.

Is my scan data stored or shared?

No, we don't store or share your scan data. All analysis happens in real-time and results are only visible to you. We respect your privacy as much as we help you protect it.

How is the security score calculated?

Our algorithm evaluates multiple factors from VirusTotal's analysis: number of malicious detections, suspicious activities, and threat intelligence from 70+ security vendors.

Can I audit password-protected sites?

Currently, SpyAudit can only analyze publicly accessible websites. We're working on solutions for authenticated pages while maintaining security standards.

How often should I audit websites?

We recommend auditing websites before visiting them for the first time, and periodically re-auditing sites you visit frequently as threats can emerge over time.
